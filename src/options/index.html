Why we ask for your email

FLoRA queries the Crossref and OpenAlex APIs to resolve article DOIs. Both services ask for a contact email so they can place your requests in their polite-access pool — meaning faster rate limits and more reliable lookups while you browse.

Your email is used only as a mailto parameter sent to Crossref & OpenAlex. It is never stored on a FLoRA server, shared, or used for anything else.
